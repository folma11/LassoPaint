# Lasso Paint Brand Guidelines

The files in `assets/` are the official Lasso Paint brand assets. Use them as supplied. Do not redraw, recolor, distort, rotate, crop, or add effects to the artwork.

## Official Logo Usage

Use `assets/logo/logo.svg` for primary brand presentations, including the README, project pages, documentation covers, and layouts with enough horizontal and vertical space for the complete lockup.

- Preserve the logo's aspect ratio.
- Keep all elements of the lockup visible.
- Place it on a background that maintains clear contrast with the artwork.
- Do not typeset a replacement wordmark or modify the tagline within the asset.

## LP Mark Usage

Use `assets/logo/mark.svg` where the full logo would be too large or repetitive, such as the compact Photoshop panel header.

- Pair it with the Lasso Paint name when the product identity may be unclear.
- Preserve its aspect ratio and supplied colors.
- Do not use the mark as a decorative pattern or combine it with other symbols.

## Icon Usage

Use only the supplied PNG icons for Photoshop UXP integration:

- `assets/icons/icon-23.png` is the 23 × 23 px panel toolbar icon.
- `assets/icons/icon-48.png` is the 48 × 48 px plugin-list icon.

Do not substitute the logo or LP mark for these purpose-built icons. Do not resample, sharpen, recolor, or add backgrounds to the icon files.

## Brand Colors

The official artwork uses the following core colors:

| Role | Color | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lasso Blue | Blue | `#1266F0` |
| Primary wordmark | Black | `#000000` |
| Secondary wordmark | Gray | `#9CA4AE` |
| Artwork background | White | `#FFFFFF` |

These values document the delivered artwork. UI colors may adapt to Photoshop's host theme, but brand artwork must not be recolored.

## Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo and LP mark equal to at least one quarter of the displayed mark height. No text, controls, borders, or other graphics should enter this area.

When the logo appears near an edge, include the same minimum space between the artwork and that edge. More space is preferred in marketing and documentation layouts.

## Minimum Icon Sizes

Use the supplied icons at their native logical sizes:

- Panel toolbar icon: minimum 23 × 23 px.
- Plugin-list icon: minimum 48 × 48 px.

Do not display either icon below its intended size. For larger brand presentations, use the SVG logo or mark instead of enlarging the PNG icons.
